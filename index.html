Phone Buddy is a mobile phone application that turns a regular smartphone into a restricted phone that kids can use to keep in touch with their parents, as well as allow parents to track where their kids are.

Phone Buddy allows for many different modes that allows you to turn off certain applications and features, allowing parents and caregivers to feel comfortable letting their young children play with their phone without the risk of deleting important information (such as photos or contacts), or accidentally calling a work colleague!

Phone Buddy is perfect for your 9 year old son who wants to walk around the block by himself, but you want to be sure he can call you (without calling anyone else!)

Phone Buddy is ideal for your todder who wants to play games while waiting with you at a Doctor's appointment, while ensuring she can't delete anything off yourphone.

With Phone Buddy in 'kid mode' you can:

  • Select which phone numbers your phone can call
  • Enable GPS tracking so you always know where your kid is
  • Set off alerts so that you know when your kid arrives/leaves a predetermined destination
  • Ensure nothing will be deleted by setting the phone to read-only mode
  • Turn your phone into a baby monitor
  • Play 'white noise' to help your baby sleep
  • Configure multiple different modes, so that you can use your phone for helping your baby go to sleep, or switch easily to a new mode so that your 10 year old can walk to school on his own comfortable in the knowledge that he can call you id needed

FAQ

What is Phone Buddy?
Phone Buddy is a smartphone application that turns a phone into a kid friendly device that allows kids to call only certain numbers, not delete anything important, play white noise, and much much more
What phones does Phone Buddy support?
Phone Buddy will be available for both IPhone and Android
Is Phone Buddy available now?
Phone Buddy is not currently available, but is expected to be available in Fall 2013
Is there a testing program I can be part of?
Phone Buddy is currently being tested by a selected group of families. If you are interested in being part of our testing program please contact us on the link above.
How much will Phone Buddy cost?
Phone buddy will be available for 99c for the basic version, with options to upgrade to more feature rich versions (price to be determined)
Who is Phone Buddy intended for?
Phone Buddy is intended to be used by busy families, who would like a simple and easy way to keep in touch with their kids.
